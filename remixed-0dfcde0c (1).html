About Me

PK

Welcome! I am Pratik Kundu, a dedicated researcher and expert in the field of Mechanical Behaviour of Materials. With years of experience in materials science and engineering, I specialize in understanding how materials respond to various mechanical forces and environmental conditions.

My work encompasses a broad spectrum of materials research, from traditional metals and alloys to advanced composites and smart materials. I am passionate about bridging the gap between theoretical understanding and practical applications in engineering design.

Through rigorous research, innovative testing methodologies, and collaborative projects, I strive to contribute to the advancement of materials science and help develop safer, more efficient, and sustainable engineering solutions.

Areas of Expertise

Fatigue & Fracture Mechanics

In-depth analysis of crack propagation, fatigue life prediction, and failure analysis of engineering materials under cyclic loading conditions.

Composite Materials

Characterization and modeling of fiber-reinforced composites, including damage mechanisms and mechanical property optimization.

Material Testing & Characterization

Advanced experimental techniques including tensile testing, impact testing, creep analysis, and microstructural evaluation.

Computational Modeling

Finite element analysis, constitutive modeling, and numerical simulation of material behavior under various loading conditions.

High-Temperature Materials

Study of material behavior at elevated temperatures, including creep, thermal fatigue, and oxidation resistance.

Failure Analysis

Investigation of material failures in service, root cause analysis, and development of prevention strategies.
